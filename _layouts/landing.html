The mission of the Northwestern Toastmasters club is to provide a mutually supportive and positive learning environment in which every member has the opportunity to develop oral communication and leadership skills, which foster self-confidence and personal growth. We provide a warm and friendly environment and welcome everyone to our club irrespective of their current public speaking or English level.

We meet every Friday in Ford ITW (Room 1-340) from 4:45 pm - 6 pm.
