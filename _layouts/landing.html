Welcome to Northwestern Multicultural Dialogue!

Sign Up

Northwestern Multicultural Dialogue is a student-led dialogue group about multiculturalism at Northwestern! Northwestern is an endless source of students from all across the globe. Do not miss this opportunity to meet people outside of your lab/department and to engage in meaningful conversations beyond schoolwork. Join the Multicultural Dialogue Group to connect with international students and create opportunities for meaningful conversations about culture differences with students from various countries.

We meet every Tuesday or Thursday from 6pm-8pm.
