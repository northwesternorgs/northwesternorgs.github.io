Summary of the Role

  • The Treasurer is responsible for Club financial policies, procedures and controls.
  • Collects dues and pays dues to Toastmasters International, and maintains records. Makes financial reports to the Club at least quarterly.
  • Receives and disburses, with approval of the Club, all Club funds.
