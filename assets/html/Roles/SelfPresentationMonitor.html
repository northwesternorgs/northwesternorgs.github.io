Summary of the Role

  • 1. Monitors, indicates and reports vocal variation.
  • 2. Looks for non-verbal mistakes: Tapping foot, knuckling fingers, twirling or touching hair, too many or too few hand gestures,clicking pen, hand in jeans or trouser pocket etc.
  • 3. Lack of facial expression.
  • 4. Out of control gestures.
  • 5. Limp handshake.

  • If you have any questions, feel free to contact the VP Education.
