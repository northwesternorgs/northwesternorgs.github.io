Summary of the Role

  • Evaluators provide verbal and written feedback to the speakers giving prepared speeches.

    • Before the meeting:
  • Print out the appropriate evaluation sheet or ask the Toastmaster or Vice President Education.

    • At the meeting:
  • Fill out the evaluation sheet.
  • Give evaluation for 2-3 minutes.
