Summary of the Role

  • The Table Topics chair delivers the Table Topics portion of the meeting.

    • Before the meeting:
  • Think of 3-4 short prompts (maybe more if there is only one prepared speech)for people to answer for 1-2 minutes during the meeting.

    • At the meeting:
  • Explain your role to the group (especially if there are new members)
  • State the prompt. Wait few seconds for people to think of an appropriate response. Choose a random person from the group to answer the question.
  • Repeat a few times (if needed)
  • Try to engage both old and new members, particularly focusing on those who do not have a job. Start with an older member, include a new member.
