Summary of the Role

  • The General Evaluator evaluates everything that takes place during the club meeting.
  • After all of the evaluation speeches, ask for the time report from the time keeper and vote.
  • Ask the grammarian, ah-um counter and self-presentation monitor to give their reports
  • Give your evaluation of the meeting based on the order of who spoke on the agenda.
