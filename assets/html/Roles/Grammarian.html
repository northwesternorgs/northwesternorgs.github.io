Summary of the Role

  • The Grammarian helps club members improve their grammar and vocabulary.
  • Before the meeting, choose a word of the day and send the word of the day and 2 example sentences with the word to the Toastmaster and the President and the Vice President Education.
  • During the meeting, explain your role to the group.
  • Announce the word of the day and read the sentences aloud, the 1st one alone and the 2nd sentence with the audience.
  • Write down the language and grammar usage of all speakers, noting incomplete sentences, mispronunciation, grammatical mistakes, non-sequiturs, malapropisms, etc
  • Keep track of who says the word of the day and how many times.
