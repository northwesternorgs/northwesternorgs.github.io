Summary of the Role

  • Give a prepared speech.

    • Before the meeting:
  • Prepare a speech according to the type of speech you want to practice.
  • Email the title of the speech and the speech number to the VP Education.
  • Feel free to approach the VP Education with any other questions.

    • At the meeting:
  • If the Toastmaster has not mentioned the length of the speech, mention the length of your speech
  • Give the speech.
