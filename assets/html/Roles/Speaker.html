Summary of the Role

  • Give a prepared speech.

    • Before the meeting:
  • Prepare a speech according to the type of speech you want to practice
  • Send title of speech to the Toastmaster and the President and the Vice President Education
  • Prepare a short introduction based on speaker's interesting facts in the Jobs tab

    • At the meeting:
  • If the Toastmaster has not mentioned the length of the speech, mention the length of your speech
  • Give the speech.
