Summary of the Role

  • The Toastmaster is a meeting's director and host.

    • Before the meeting:
  • Send your thought of the day to the Toastmaster and these people (Arindam: arindampaul.bits@gmail.com,Reda: reda.bahrani@gmail.com)
  • Remind each speaker to get in touch with their respective evaluator to share their personal objectives besides the speech objectives.
  • Prepare a short introduction based on speaker's interesting facts in the Jobs tab

    • At the meeting:
  • Arrive early.
  • Wait for the president to introduce you.
  • Start the meeting.
  • Remind people to clap and explain why this is important.
  • Remind people to shake hands and explain why this is important.
  • Introduce the grammarian, ah/um counter, table topics chair, time keeper, vote counter.
  • Follow the items on the agenda.
  • After all of the table topics talks and prepared speeches, ask for the time report from the time keeper and vote.
  • Announce the winners of the votes.
