Summary of the Role

  • 1. The TimeKeeper-VoteCounter is responsible for monitoring the time of speakers and counting the votes.
  • 2. At the beginning of the meeting, explain your role to the group.
  • 3. Keep track of the time for table topics (1-2 minutes), speeches (usually 5-7 minutes, check with the Toastermaster), and evaluator (2-3 minutes)
  • 4. Give warnings before people go over time.
  • 5. At the end of each segment, mention the time taken by each speaker.
  • 6. At the end of each segment (table topics, prepared speeches and evaluation speeches),ask people to close their eyes and take the votes by raising of hands.
  • 7. When asked to reveal the Table Topics winner by the Toastmaster/Chair, enthusiastically announce the winner and present them with a medal.
